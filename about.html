    Our Story

    We started our journey in 2021 with the hope of creating a space where people can experience and learn about the universe in their everyday lives. Our café offers a unique experience where customers can enjoy planetarium shows while relaxing with a cup of tea. We also offer home projectors, allowing our guests to bring the beauty of the night sky into their own homes. The name "Astraea" comes from a goddess in Greek mythology. She was said to be the last deity to leave the human world, holding onto her belief in humanity’s goodness until the very end. Even now, she is believed to watch over us, hoping for the return of a peaceful world. We chose her name for our café as a reflection of that same hope.
